One website. Real scheduling. Less friction.

A modern replacement for three separate patient-facing sites.

Prepared for Chiropractic Associates & The Rub Club — Paris, TX and Sulphur Springs, TX.

Where things stand today

The practice is effectively one operation with multiple entry points, but the public web is split across three different domains. Patients and Google each have to “pick a door,” and it is easy for hours, phone numbers, and messaging to drift out of sync between them.

https://www.chiropracticparistexas.com/
https://www.massageparistexas.com/
https://www.chiropracticsulphursprings.com/

What we are proposing

One primary website that clearly explains both brands, both locations, and both service lines — chiropractic and massage — with a single, coherent path from “I need help” to “I’m booked.”

Behind that, a scheduler and staff tools (not just a contact form): real availability, confirmed appointments, notifications, and an internal view of the schedule so the front desk spends less time on phone tag.

Why consolidate

  • Clarity for patients: one trusted place for services, hours, insurance, forms, and booking.
  • Stronger marketing: one domain earns the reviews, links, and search relevance instead of splitting them three ways.
  • Easier maintenance: update hours or providers once, not across three separate admin logins.

Why add real scheduling

  • Fewer missed connections: patients see open times and commit without waiting for a callback.
  • Staff alignment: the schedule becomes a shared source of truth, not sticky notes and voicemails.
  • Room to grow: the same platform can evolve (reminders, policies, reporting) because you own the software path.
Bottom line: you move from three brochure sites (read-only marketing on separate hosts) to one patient experience backed by operational software your team actually uses every day.

If you want to move forward, the next step is a short walkthrough of the new site and scheduler on a demo link, then a simple cutover plan for domains, phones on marketing materials, and Search Console.
